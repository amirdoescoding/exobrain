What you get

Everything your brain needs, automated.

Three powerful features that work together to keep you informed and organized without lifting a finger.

Daily Brief

Every morning ExoBrain searches the web — weather, world news, AI & tech, macroeconomics, local news — and writes it all to a structured daily note in your vault.

runs at 9 AM automatically

Summarizer

Drop a YouTube link, podcast URL, or PDF. ExoBrain writes a Wikipedia-style summary with TLDR, timestamps, key quotes, and linked concepts — saved to your library.

YouTube · podcasts · PDFs

Knowledge Library

Every article, source, and public figure mentioned in your briefs gets auto-catalogued. Your vault grows smarter every single day — without you doing anything.

Setup

Three steps. Five minutes.

No accounts to create. No dashboards to configure. Just a terminal and a couple of questions.

01

Run one command

Open your terminal and run npx exobrain. That's it — no cloning, no downloading, no installation steps.

02

Answer 5 questions

The setup wizard asks for your vault location, name, city, timezone, and preferred brief time. Press Enter to accept smart defaults.

03

Open Obsidian

Point Obsidian at your new vault, enable the Unread Indicator plugin, and you're done. Your first brief arrives tomorrow at 9 AM.
